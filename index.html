About TNAU

Established in 1971, Tamil Nadu Agricultural University is a globally recognized center of excellence. It offers 14 UG, 34 PG, and 29 doctoral degrees, serving as a hub for agricultural innovation with 19 constituent colleges and 40 research stations.

About AEC & RI

Agricultural Engineering College and Research Institute is a leading institution in India. It conducts advanced research in farm machinery, food processing, renewable energy, and environmental engineering to enhance sustainability.

Dept of Food Process Engineering
